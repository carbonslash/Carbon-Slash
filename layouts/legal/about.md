## Our Mission

Carbon Slash is Africa's premier organisation dedicated to tracking and analyzing the continent's transition to sustainable energy. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, our mission is to provide sharp, insightful, and locally relevant reporting on electric mobility, renewable energy innovation, and policy decisions that shape Africa's future.

## Our Vision

We envision a future where Africa leads the world in adopting clean, equitable energy systems. We aim to be the trusted voice and essential resource for investors, policymakers, and innovators driving this change across the continent.
