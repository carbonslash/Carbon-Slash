# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: November 15, 2025**
#
**Last Updated: November 15, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Carbon Slash** (the **Data Controller**), in compliance with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA)**, collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you and automatically as you use the Site. The legal basis for processing your data is primarily **Consent** (for subscriptions) and **Legitimate Interests** (for site security and analytics).

#### (a). Personal Data Provided Directly

We collect Personal Data when you voluntarily provide it to us, primarily through the **Newsletter Subscription Form**.

* **Email Address:** Collected when you subscribe to our newsletter.
* **Other Information:** Any other information you voluntarily submit via contact forms or direct email correspondence.

#### (b). Information We Collect Automatically

We use third-party services like **Google Analytics** to understand how the Site is being used. These tools use **Cookies** and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy for details).

* **Usage Data:** Information about your interaction with the Site (e.g., pages viewed, time spent, links clicked).
* **Technical Data:** Your computer's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser version, device type, and operating system.

## 2. Data Sharing and Transfer

We **do not sell** your Personal Data. We share it only with trusted third parties who assist us in operating the Site, ensuring they comply with the DPA:

* **Service Providers:** Third-party companies (e.g., newsletter platform) who process data on our behalf as **Data Processors**.
* **Transfer of Data:** Your data may be stored or processed outside of Kenya. Where this occurs, we ensure that appropriate safeguards, as required by the DPA, are in place (e.g., adequate protection standards).
* **Legal Compliance:** We will disclose your data where required by the laws of the **Republic of Kenya**, or in response to valid requests by a public authority (e.g., the Office of the Data Commissioner, Kenyan courts).

## 3. Your Rights under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

As a data subject under the DPA, you have the right to:

* **Be Informed** of the use of your Personal Data.
* **Access** your Personal Data.
* **Object** to the processing of all or part of your Personal Data.
* **Rectify** false or misleading Personal Data.
* **Erase** Personal Data that is no longer necessary (the right to be forgotten).
* **Withdraw Consent** at any time (e.g., by unsubscribing).

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. If you believe your rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Office of the Data Commissioner (ODC)**.
