# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date: November 15, 2025**
#
**Last Updated: November 15, 2025**

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how **Carbon Slash** uses them on our website. This policy is provided in line with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019**, which requires that we obtain explicit consent for the processing of Personal Data collected via tracking technologies.

## 1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help the website function efficiently and provide us with anonymized data on how the Site is used.

## 2. Consent to Use Cookies (DPA Compliance)

By continuing to use our Site after viewing the cookie notification banner, you are providing your **explicit consent** for the placement of non-essential cookies (such as analytics cookies) on your device, in compliance with the DPA's requirement for lawful processing.

## 3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

* **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Essential for the basic operation of the Site (e.g., maintaining security). **These do not require consent.**
* **Performance and Analytics Cookies:** Used via third-party services like Google Analytics to measure and analyze visitor traffic and improve the user experience. **These require your explicit consent.**

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

Our Site uses **Google Analytics**, a third-party service that places cookies on your device. We ensure that this processing adheres to the data protection principles of the DPA, and the data collected is used solely to create statistical reports about website activity.

## 5. Managing Your Choices

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. You can manage or delete cookies:

* **Browser Controls:** Most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking cookies may impact the full functionality of the Site.
* **Third-Party Opt-Out:** You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking through mechanisms provided by Google.
