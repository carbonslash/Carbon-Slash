## The Promise of Lithium-Ion's Successor

Traditional liquid lithium-ion batteries have long dominated the market, but their performance plateau and safety concerns are pushing manufacturers toward a new frontier. Solid-state technology eliminates the flammable liquid electrolyte, replacing it with a solid material that can be safer and more energy-dense.
