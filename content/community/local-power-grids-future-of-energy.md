## Resilience Through Decentralization

The vulnerability of large, centralized power grids during extreme weather events has highlighted the necessity of local power generation. Microgrids, often powered by solar and storage, can disconnect from the main grid to operate independently, providing critical power to hospitals and emergency centers.
